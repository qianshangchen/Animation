11.11 Globe Shopping Festival in China from 2009-2017

  • 2009, Taobao of Alibaba host the first Globe Shopping Festival, and the amount of sales is 0.052 Billion RMB.

    2009-11-11

  • 2010, the amount of sales of Tmall Globe Shopping Festival reached to 0.936 Billion RMB.

    2010-11-11

  • 2011, the amount of sales of Tmall Globe Shopping Festival reached to 3.36 Billion RMB already.

    2011-11-11

  • 2012, the amount of sales of Tmall Globe Shopping Festival have rapidly increased due to Aliply, reached 19.1 Biliion RMB.

    2012-11-11

  • 2013, Taobao "11,11"(the same day of Globe Shopping Festival) only used 55 second to break through 0.1 billion RMB. Eventually it reached 35.2 Billion RMB

    2013-11-11

  • 2014, the amount of sales of Tmall Globe Shopping Festival was 57.1 billion RMB.

    2014-11-11

  • 2015, till midnight of 11/11, the amount of sales of Tmall Globe Shopping Festival was 91.217 billion RMB.

    2015-11-11

  • 2016, the amount of sales of Tmall Globe Shopping Festival was 12.07 billion RMB.

    2016-11-11

  • 2017, the amount of sales of Tmall Globe Shopping Festival was 168.2 billion RMB

    2017-11-11
